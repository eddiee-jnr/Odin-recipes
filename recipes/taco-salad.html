Taco Salad

This Taco Salad is quick, flavorful, and hearty. It's a total breeze to make and a dinner everyone can agree on. It is a delectable mash-up of fresh lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and lean ground beef. It skips the hassle of stuffing a shell with taco salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 (16 ounce) can chili beans
  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle French dressing
  • 1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) package tortilla chips
  • 1 head iceberg lettuce, shredded
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • Â½ cup prepared salsa
  • 4 tablespoons sour cream

Directions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain and discard grease.
  2. Stir in chili beans, French-style dressing, and seasoning mix. Fill the empty dressing bottle 2/3 full of water and add to the skillet. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Crush the bag of chips, open the bag, and toss crushed chips into a large bowl.
  4. Add cheese, and tomatoes. Pour meat mixture into the bowl; mix well.
  5. Fill a bowl with lettuce. Top with meat mixture, salsa, and sour cream. Serve warm and enjoy!

Nutrition Fact

In genral, taco salad offers approximately 596 calories, 34g of total fat, 80mg cholesterol, 1,000mg sodium, 48g of carbohydrates, 24g of protein.
It's also a good source of vitamins A and C, calcium, and iron.
