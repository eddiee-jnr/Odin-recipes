Veggie Egg white Omelette

A vegetable egg white omelettethat can be made with a variety of veggies and cheeses.
This fluffy omelet is so easy to make and it tastes so good! It's a pretty good quick breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1/4 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup baby spinach
  • a pinch of garrlic powder for flavor
  • 3/4 cup egg whites
  • 2 tablespoons favorite cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2-3 fresh basil leaves (optional)

Instructions for Preparing

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan, saut√© onion and bell pepper until softened, add spinach, stir, and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Pour egg whites over vegetables, gently lifting edges with a rubber spatula to allow liquid to run under and cook completely.
  3. After the eggs have set, sprinkle your cheese and basil on one half of the omelette and fold it in half using a spatula.
  4. Transfer to a plate and serve warm. Garnish with avocado and/or fresh herbs if desired.

Pro tips

  • Cook vegetables in eggs instead of cooking them separately and stuff them with the filling.
  • To ensure the omelet is fully cooked on both sides, flip it before folding it.

Nutrition Fact

The vegetable egg white omelette has 137 calories per serving, 6.4g total fat, 16mg cholesterol, 811mg sodium, 7.7g total carbohydrates, and 12g protein.
